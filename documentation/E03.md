> What is the usable (host) address range of 192.168.32.0/24? What is the broadcast address? What is the subnet address?


```
11111111 . 11111111 . 11111111 . 00000000    -> BIN
nnnnnnnn . nnnnnnnn . nnnnnnnn . hhhhhhhh

|BIN             DEC|
```
